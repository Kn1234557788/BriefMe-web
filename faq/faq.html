Frequently Asked Question

  • 1. How do I record a meeting?

    To record a meeting, open the app and tap the "BriefMe AI" icon at the bottom and click on "start recording".


  • 2. How can I access my meeting summaries?

    Your meeting summaries are available on the home screen. Tap on any summary to view the details.


  • 3. How do I select the template for summarization?

    Select the appropriate template based on the preview from the template selection screen after uploading the recording.


  • 4. Can I share the summary with others?

    Yes the summaries generated can be shared with anyone. Go to a particular summary and click on share icon on the top right, now you can select to whom you want to share and via which platform you want to share.


  • 5. Can I upload my already recorded audio/videos?

    Yes you can upload your already recorded audio, videos from the upload section from the sidebar. Click on "Upload", give it a title, select template and select an option from Audio/Video, upload a file by clicking on "Upload File" and click on "upload" button.
